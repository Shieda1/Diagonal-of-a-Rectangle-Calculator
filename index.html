What is the diagonal of a rectangle?
The diagonal of a rectangle is a line segment that connects any two of a rectangle's non-adjacent vertices. A diagonal divides a rectangle into two equal triangles, How to calculate diagonal of a rectangle?
To calculate diagonal of a rectangle you should use the following formula:
d = √(h² + w²)
where:
d = diagonal
h = height
w = width
